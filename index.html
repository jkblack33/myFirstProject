Five Step Plan to Take Over The World

Step One: Start a Corporation

Corporations are the best thing in the world. They are considered a "legal person" and can shield you from liability from the other 99%. And the best thing about corporations:

MONEY.

Lots and lots and lots of money.

Use your Corporation to make money and shield your wealth so you can invest it to fuel your ambitions.

Step Two: Buy Up Everything

Use your ill-gotten gains to buy up everything. Everything.

Hospitals. Schools. Pharmaceutical Companies. Grocery Stores. You name it.

Just approach them and offer a deal. Be like: "Hey, I'm buying you out. So, yeah."

And if they refuse, just kneecap em.

They can't go after you because the "Corporation" did it. Not you. In fact, it was a subsidiary. Who cares.

Step Three: Buy the Government

Find and up and coming young politian and fund his campaign. Get him a seat in office.

Ideally he'd be married. And has a vice. You can use this.

Buy him an escort. Throw a party. Take pictures.

Now you own him.

You're welcome.

Step Four: Start A Private Military Company

Do Whatever You Want

Now you got money, you own precious resources, you own the government and you own your own private army.
